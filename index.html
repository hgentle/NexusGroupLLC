Overview

Nexus Group’s Equipment Maintenance Management Solution (EMMS) provides dynamic full-service equipment maintenance and support for a broad range of specialized equipment across various manufacturers. Unique to Nexus, we incorporate the customer’s preferred service providers, including the Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM) and regional ISOs, as part of our comprehensive solution.

As a federal contractor with over 20 years combined experience, Nexus supports a broad range of equipment types in healthcare, laboratory, government, and university environments. Nexus provides a single point of contact for managing services. Our solution provides comprehensive coverage that includes all costs associated with on-site service, repairs, parts, labor, travel, emergency repair, preventative maintenance, and technical support.

With over 20 years of combined experience working with clinical engineering staff, OEMs, ISO certified providers, strategic sourcing professionals, parts distributors, technology integrators, and biomedical teams, Nexus Group integrates the expertise of service providers across multiple disciplines to deliver efficient and responsive equipment maintenance management services. Nexus Group’s multi-vendor solution offers a unified workflow, service standardization, simplified processes, technician redundancies, and significant cost savings compared to traditional service agreements.

Insights

Research Laboratory

Research and analytical laboratories rely on highly proprietary equipment where only the Original Equipment Manufacturer is authorized to access software and service all aspects of the equipment. This generally limits service options and forces laboratories to issue multiple agreements to respective manufacturers with varying processes, workflows, ...and inconsistent expiration dates. Within procurement, this creates single source acquisitions that are difficult to compete and validate for cost reasonableness. Nexus Group offers a dynamic solution that leverages the Original Equipment Manufacturers for all proprietary service needs while incorporating regional ISOs for routine service when possible to promote a highly responsive hybrid solution. Nexus Group utilizes only qualified manufacturer technicians across multiple proprietary equipment types under a single agreement to optimize service request workflows and significantly reduce maintenance costs.
